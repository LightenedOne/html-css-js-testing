FAQ

Are your commissions open?

You can find that out here! You can also view my queue here!

What programs do you use?

I use an 11-inch iPad Pro with Procreate and Clip Studio Paint!

Can I use your art as a reference?

Sure, as long as it isn't a commission! If you need to heavily reference one of my pieces, please list a credit for me!

Can I use your art?

If you aren't using it commerically and it isn't a commissions or art of my characters, sure!

Can I repost your art?

I have a list of sites I post my art on; if you want to post it somewhere that isn't on the list, you can post it as long as you credit.

Why am I blocked?

If you're blocked, it's for a reason and it's going to stay that way. Please don't try to contact me.

Do you take requests?

Sometimes I post on Twitter, asking for suggestions on things to draw - other than that, I don't take requests.
