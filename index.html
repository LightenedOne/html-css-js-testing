FAQ

Are your commissions open?

You can find that out here! You can also view a list of my queue here!

Will you work for free/exposure?

Absolutely not! Art is my full time job and I take it very seriously. If you wouldn't work for hours at a time for free, don't expect an artist to do so.

Will you draw ____?

I have a list of things I will and won't draw. If something isn't on that list, there's a good chance I'm willing to draw it.

Can I get art involving one of your characters?

Maybe! It depends on what you want. Send me a message and we can talk about it!

Do you do art trades?

Sometimes! I decide on a case-by-case basis, send me a message and we can talk about it!
